Located in north Richmond, an industrial suburb in British Columbia's Lower Mainland, LT Custom machining specializes in light (up to 12" diameter) precision machining. Our modern CNC machining equipment will efficiently meet your re- quirements for cost-effective high-quality machined parts.

Our qualified machining team of dedicated and enthusiastic individuals, is focussed on meeting our customers’ needs. We understand quality and the need to meet delivery commitments.

Working for our customers in the petro- chemical, forestry, construction, architec- tural and marine industries, we have proven our ability to produce complex, tight-toleranced parts using a variety of materials.
